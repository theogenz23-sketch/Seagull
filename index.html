About Us

At the heart of Seagull Compass is Rajalakshmi Ramesh ( Raji ) , a coach, behavioral trainer, and wellness guide. With over 15 years of experience, she helps people grow with clarity, compassion, and purpose.

Her journey blends two worlds: the structure of corporate leadership and the depth of holistic healing. Using tools like emotional intelligence, mindful coaching, Reiki, and team development, she holds space for change that feels real and lasting.

Raji’s vision is simple: to walk with you as you rediscover your strengths, shift your mindset, and come home to a fuller version of yourself. At Seagull Compass, we believe growth happens when skill, soul, and self-awareness move in harmony.

The Heart Behind Seagull Compass

A Special Note: My Mentor Rajalakshmi

During one of my lowest career moments, Rajalakshmi entered my life as a mentor. My dream of becoming an airline pilot was hanging by a thin thread . I was failing on all fronts, turning into a recluse, and heading toward depression. Rajalakshmi’s first session immediately put my jangling nerves at ease. She is a keen observer, considerate, and an incredibly patient listener who never interrupted me. Her ability to connect emotionally helped me restart my social interactions and rebuild my confidence.

Thanks to her positive reinforcement, I regained the courage to face my challenges. She never highlighted my negatives or those I interacted with, instead focusing on my strengths. Eventually, I cleared my tests and achieved my goal of becoming a pilot. The lessons I learned from her not only helped me succeed professionally but also enriched my daily life, reminding me of the power of patience, kindness, and positive reinforcement.

Thank you, Rajalakshmi. Your guidance was a quiet yet powerful catalyst in my journey of growth.
